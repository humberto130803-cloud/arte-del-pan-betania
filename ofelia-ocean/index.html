About

Ofelia Méndez

Panamanian entrepreneur with a distinguished career spanning aviation, automotive, and banking. As founding Vice President of A.D.E.A.N. at Paitilla Airport and later leading MMC Panamá, she built a legacy of leadership across industries.

Driven by her deep connection to the sea, Ofelia created a brand that transforms the ocean's beauty into wearable art — a tribute to nature and a call to protect our seas.
